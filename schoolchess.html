To set up a chessboard, place the board so a light-colored square is in the bottom-right corner. Next, place the pawns on the second row. For the back row, put the rooks in the corners, followed by the knights, then the bishops. Place the queen on the central square of her color (white queen on light square \(d1\), black queen on dark square \(d8\)), and the king in the remaining spot next to her.
